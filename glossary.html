Glossary of Terms in Dragon Warrior Racing

TermMeaning
AK, AK47Usually the Armored Knight. Sometimes can refer to the Axe Knight. Both have the initials "AK"
Big CCharlock Castle. The walls form a C, with the Rainbow Drop to be placed in the moat at the opening of the C.
Big StickErdrick's Sword, the strongest melee weapon in the game. Occasionaly the Bamboo Pole, the weakest weapon other than your fists.
BonkTo run into impassible terrian such as rivers, walls, and NPCs. The game makes a "bonk" sound when this happens.
Bonus CaveThe cave that contains the Stones of Sunlight in the base game. It contains an old man with a free chest in it.
CountTo count the tiles out for the Search Tile without using Gwaelin's Love. See also: Gwaelin's Love, Reverse Count
Death WarpTo intentionally take a death to go back to Tantagel Castle.
Deep GraveThe two key locked chests below the top of the Grave of Garinham
Dive, The Dive1. (The Dive) To go into Charlock Castle to attempt to defeat the Dragonlord. 2. To go into any location, usually a dungeon, eg. I dived Mountain for Erdrick's Sword
DoubleTo attack the Dragonlord twice in a row in the final fight. See also Manufactured Double
French VanillaAn item that's in the same area, but not the exact same spot. For example, having the Silver Harp in the top three of Grave instead of the final chest. See also: Vanilla
Fun PoliceThe Red Dragon. Hardest enemy to run from, when they pull you over, the fun stops.
Gold GrindTo take a chest containing gold multiple times. See here for various methods
GPSGwaelin's Love, aka the Gwaelin Positioning System (GPS). Tells you your position relative to Tantagel Castle
Healwalking1. The one hp per step given by Erdrick's Armor, or one hp per four steps by the Magic Armor 2. To gain health by using the healwalking power.
Judge's TableThe chest in Charlock Castle that normally contains Erdrick's Sword. It's out of the way from the Dragonlord, and is usually the last location checked in Charlock, if needed.
Key LockedAny item locked behind a door requiring a key. This technically includes the item in Rimuldar, even though keys are purchasable in Rimuldar
Manufactured DoubleTo attack the Dragonlord twice despite HP being low enough to risk death. See also Double
MinibossThe forced fight just before the Dragonlord. See: Spike Tile.
Reverse CountTo count the tiles out for the search spot, taking advantage of the fact that the Dragon Warrior map is 120x120. If you got coordinates of 119,119, you'd know the search item is in the exact opposite corner from Tantagel. (0,0 is a valid location for the search item)
Search TileA tile in Kol, Hauksness or on the overworld that can contain an item. In vanilla, these contain the Fairy Flute, Erdrick's Armor and Erdrick's Token respectively.
Spike TileA tile that contains a forced fight. One is in Hauksness guarding the possible item there, one is in the Swamp Cave guarding Princess Gwaelin, one is just before the Dragonlord.
TreasuryThe key locked chests in either Tantagel Castle or Charlock Castle
Vanilla1. The base game of Dragon Warrior. 2.An item that is found in the same place it normally is in the base game. Often accompined by the expression "Is this even randomized?"
Wombo ComboAn enemy with both SLEEP and Dragonlord 2 breath. Junior Wombo, Diet Wombo: SLEEP and HURTMORE or HURT
ZoneAn area containing a set group of monsters. There are several zones on the overworld, as well as the various caves and Charlock itself.
