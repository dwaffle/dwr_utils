Getting Started

You will need:
    a copy of the US version of Dragon Warrior Randomzier.
    An NES emulator. I personally use BizHawk.
    You can find these using your favorite search engine.
Go to dwrandomizer.com load in the ROM where it asks for the Rom File, and click Randomize! You'll be given an NES rom, this is your randomized game.
The Checksum that's also generated is used for generating the same seed again, for example, in races.
Beginning a game:
The goal is the same as it is in regular Dragon Warrior: find the Silver Harp, trade it in for the Staff of Rain, find the Stones of Sunlight and Erdrick's Token, and defeat the Dragonlord.
Along the way, you'll need to pick up Erdrick's Armor, most likely need Erdrick's Sword, and acquire enough HP, MP and Attack Power.
However, all the chests have been randomized, as well as the search spots in Kol, Hauksness and the coordinates.
Other things to know:
  • Monsters use the increased experience values from the remakes.
  • Torches can be thrown at enemies for 6-9 damage. Fairy Waters can be thrown for 8-16 damage.
  • All spells will be granted by level 16
  • Monsters have skills and spells shuffled. You can have a blue slime with Dragonlord breath, or a Red Dragon with HURT!
  • Red Dragons and Golems have had their experience value increased to 350
  • Goldmen have had their gold value increased to 650
  • Enemies with spells have roll between 0-9/16 STOPSPELL resistance rolled at the start of the game, with more powerful enemies having a higher maximum
